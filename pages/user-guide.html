User Guide

Official usage guide and marketplace terms for UniMart users.

Access Requirements

User Information Requirement

  • Before using buying or selling functions, users must provide accurate personal information.
  • A valid WeChat contact must be maintained for communication purposes.
  • Failure to provide or maintain accurate information may result in restricted access or account suspension.

Policy Agreement Notice

By using UniMart, you agree to comply with platform policies and guidelines. Please review and understand these terms before proceeding.

How to Buy
  1. Browse or Search for Items: Explore listings or use search to find specific items.
  2. Review Item Details: Check price, condition, images, and description carefully.
  3. Contact the Seller: Scan the listing QR code to contact the seller directly. WeChat is recommended.
  4. Discuss and Agree on Terms: Confirm details, pricing, and meeting arrangements clearly.
  5. Arrange a Meetup: Set a suitable meeting time and location, preferably on campus.
  6. Complete the Transaction: Inspect the item before payment and proceed only when satisfied.

Recommendation: Compare multiple listings and always inspect items carefully before payment.

How to Sell
  1. Create a Listing: Provide accurate item name, price, category, condition, description, images, and contact QR code.
  2. Publish Your Listing: Once submitted, your listing becomes visible to other users.
  3. Respond to Inquiries: Buyers may contact you through QR/WeChat. Respond politely and provide extra details when needed.
  4. Arrange a Meetup: After agreement, choose a suitable campus time and location.
  5. Complete the Transaction: Deliver the item and receive payment directly.
  6. Mark Item as Sold: Update status promptly after completion.

Tip: Clear images, complete descriptions, and updated listing status improve visibility and conversion.

My Sales (Listing Management)
  • Edit Listing: You can update price, description, images, and other listing details at any time.
  • Mark as Sold: Once sold, update listing status to Sold to keep marketplace results accurate.
  • Maintain Listing Quality: Promptly mark sold items or withdraw inactive listings to improve user experience.
