User Guide

Official usage guide and marketplace terms for UniMart users.

Introduction

UniMart is a campus marketplace designed to help students buy and sell items in a more organized and efficient way.

The platform improves visibility and convenience compared to traditional group-based trading.

How to Sell

Create a Listing

Provide clear details including name, price, category, condition, description, images, and contact QR code.

Publish Your Listing

Your item becomes visible to other users on the platform.

Respond to Inquiries

Buyers contact you via QR code. Use WeChat for smooth communication.

Arrange Meetup

Agree on a time and location, preferably within campus.

Complete Transaction

Meet buyer, deliver item, and receive payment.

Mark as Sold

Update status via My Sales page after completion.

  • Adjust price or improve description if item is not selling.
  • Clear images increase selling speed.

How to Buy

Browse or Search

Explore listings or search for specific items.

Review Details

Check price, condition, images, and description carefully.

Contact Seller

Scan QR code and communicate via WeChat.

Agree on Terms

Confirm price, details, and meeting arrangement.

Arrange Meetup

Set a convenient time and location.

Complete Transaction

Inspect item and proceed with payment.

  • Compare multiple listings before buying.
  • Always inspect items before payment.

My Sales (Listing Management)

Edit Listing

Update price, description, images anytime.

Mark as Sold

Update status after successful sale.

Withdraw Listing

Remove listing if item is no longer available.

  • Keep listings updated to improve visibility.
  • Remove inactive listings for better experience.

Terms of Use & Marketplace Policy Highlights

Access & Eligibility

  • Provide accurate personal information and maintain a valid WeChat contact.
  • Agree to Terms before buying or selling.
  • Comply with applicable laws and university regulations.

Seller & Buyer Responsibilities

  • Sellers must post truthful listings, clear images, and update status promptly.
  • Buyers must review details, verify condition/authenticity, and inspect before payment.
  • All users must communicate respectfully and avoid fraud, abuse, or fake accounts.

Safety, Payments & Liability

Payments and Transaction Risk

  • UniMart does not process payments or provide escrow.
  • Users choose payment methods and verify payments at their own risk.
  • UniMart is not liable for payment disputes, scams, non-payment, or chargebacks.

Safety & Enforcement

  • Meet in public campus locations and exercise caution with unfamiliar users.
  • Illegal, counterfeit, unsafe, or restricted items are prohibited.
  • UniMart may remove listings or suspend accounts for policy violations.

Welcome to UniMart

Before you start buying or selling, please read the Terms of Use & Marketplace Policy and agree in your profile. This helps keep transactions safe and clear for everyone.
